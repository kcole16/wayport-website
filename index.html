Compliance & Regulation

Wayport maintains and implements robust BSA/AML and OFAC compliance policies and procedures. All counterparties undergo KYB screening, including KYC verification of UBOs and control persons, as well as sanctions and PEP screening. Wayport utilizes blockchain analytics for transaction monitoring and wallet screening. Wayport does not serve clients from jurisdictions designated as high-risk by the Financial Action Task Force. Wayport does not currently serve U.S. persons.
